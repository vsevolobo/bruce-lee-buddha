# Quick Start Guide

Welcome to the Quick Start Guide for the Bruce Lee Buddha application. This guide will help you get started with the PWA (Progressive Web App) on Android, importing your first configuration, connecting to the server, and basic troubleshooting tips.

## Opening PWA on Android
1. Open your preferred web browser on your Android device.
2. Enter the URL of the Bruce Lee Buddha app.
3. Tap the menu (three dots) in the top right corner of your browser.
4. Select 'Add to Home screen'.
5. Follow the prompts to add the PWA to your home screen.
6. Open the PWA from your home screen like any other app.

## Importing Your First Config
1. Launch the Bruce Lee Buddha PWA on your device.
2. Navigate to the settings menu within the app.
3. Look for the 'Import Configuration' option.
4. Select and upload your configuration file (usually in JSON format).
5. Confirm the import and wait for the application to process your settings.

## Connecting to Server
1. Tap on the 'Settings' option within the app.
2. Locate the 'Server Connection' settings.
3. Enter the server address and port number provided by your administrator.
4. Click 'Connect' and wait for the confirmation message.
5. Make sure you have a stable internet connection to ensure a successful connection.

## Basic Troubleshooting
- **Cannot Open App**: Ensure you have a stable internet connection and that you've added the app to your home screen correctly.
- **Import Errors**: Double-check that your configuration file is in the correct format (JSON). Make sure there are no syntax errors in the file.
- **Server Connection Issues**: Verify the server address and port number. If the problem persists, contact your administrator for assistance.

If you encounter any further issues, please refer to the FAQ section in the app or contact support.
